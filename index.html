About SSN

Sprawling over a lush 250 acre campus, SSN provides its students with the best possible infrastructure for learning,and has created an environment that stimulates intellectual growth and innovation. Prioritizing the promotion extra and co curricular activities alike alongside academics to ensure holistic development of every one of its students, is what sets SSN apart from other colleges in India. In addition to world-class teaching and infrastructure, SSN has been voted one of the most beautiful campuses in India, providing an incomparable experience to its students and faculty.
